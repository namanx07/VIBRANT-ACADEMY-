Who We Are

About Vibrant Academy

A premier institution shaping the future leaders of tomorrow

Vibrant Academy, Deoria is a premier English medium CBSE school of Eastern Uttar Pradesh. Established in 2021 by GKS Vibrant Academy Pvt. Ltd., the school is committed to delivering quality education from Class 5th to 12th with Maths, Biology & Commerce streams.

🎯 Aim & Objective

The school primarily aims at the integral development of every child — intellectual, mental and spiritual — to shape them into responsible, patriotic and disciplined citizens who serve the nation with compassion and excellence.

Admission Process & Guidelines

Simple, transparent and parent-friendly admission process

📋 Admission Criteria

  • 📅 New admissions are taken in February, based on an interaction session.
  • 👨‍👩‍👦 Both parents must attend the admission interaction.
  • 💰 Registration Fee: ₹500 (non-refundable).
  • 📄 Documents required: Transfer Certificate + Birth Certificate.
  • ⚠️ Fee once paid is non-refundable under any circumstances.
Enquire Now

👨‍👩‍👧 Guidelines for Parents

  • ✅ Maximum co-operation expected in cleanliness and discipline of the campus.
  • ✅ Fees must be paid by due date. Pending dues will result in students not being allowed to appear in exams.
  • ✅ All communication to be addressed to the Principal only.
  • ✅ No parent-teacher meetings during school hours.
  • ✅ Parents must attend all scheduled Parent-Teacher Meetings without fail.
>📏 Rules for Students — Discipline & Uniform

👧 For Girls

  • 👗 Proper uniform to be worn at all times.
  • 💇‍♀️ Hair must be in two plaits only.
  • 💎 Only small earrings are permitted.

👦 For Boys

  • 👔 Proper uniform to be worn at all times.
  • 💈 Side-parted short hair is mandatory.
  • 👞 Laced black shoes only to be worn.
📌 Leave note must be approved by the Principal before any absence.
